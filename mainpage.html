Who is he?

Warren Buffet

Warren Buffett is an American businessman, investor and philanthropist. He is one of the most successful investors in the world. Warren Buffett is the chairman, CEO and largest shareholder of Berkshire Hathaway, and he is ranked among the world's wealthiest people.In 2012, Time named Buffett one of the world's most influential people. At age of 11, he bought three shares of Cities Service Preferred for himself, and three for his sister and was the first time he invested from then he starts growing.
